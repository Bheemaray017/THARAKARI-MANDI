About Tharakari Mandi

At Tharakari Mandi, we connect you directly with local farmers to bring you the freshest, most nutritious produce possible. Our platform supports small-scale farmers while providing you with quality vegetables and fruits.

We don't use any synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, and our produce is always fresh from the farm. Our vegetables are harvested at peak ripeness and delivered to you within hours of picking.
